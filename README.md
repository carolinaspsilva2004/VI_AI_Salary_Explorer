# Information Visualization — Project

## Global Salary Trends in AI & Data

---

## Project Description
This project was developed for the course **Information Visualization** and focuses on the **analysis and visualization of global salary trends** in the fields of **Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Machine Learning, Analytics, and Data Engineering**.

Through interactive and informative visualizations, the project explores **how salaries evolve over time** and how factors such as **experience level, geographic location, employment type, company size, and work arrangement (remote, hybrid, or on-site)** influence compensation.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze global salary trends in AI & Data-related roles between 2020 and 2025  
- Compare salaries across **job roles, professional categories, and experience levels**  
- Identify geographic patterns and assess the impact of remote work  
- Support **career decisions** and **compensation strategies** through data-driven insights  

---

## Data
- **Sources:** 365DataScience, AIJobs.net, Wellfound (formerly AngelList), Payscale, KDnuggets, ZipRecruiter  
- **Key variables:**
  - Year
  - Job title and job category
  - Experience level
  - Employment type
  - Company size and location
  - Employee residence
  - Remote work ratio
  - Annual salary (normalized to EUR)

---

## Target Users
- **Professionals and students** in Data Science, AI, and Analytics fields  
- **Recruiters and HR teams** seeking salary benchmarking insights  
- **Companies and startups** aiming to design competitive and fair compensation strategies
